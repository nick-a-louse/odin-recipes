Ultimate spaghetti carbonara recipe

Description

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an Italian favourite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

Ingredients

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. STEP 1: Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. STEP 2: Finely chop the 100g pancetta, having first removed any rind. Finely grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together.
  3. STEP 3: Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl and season with a little freshly grated black pepper. Set everything aside.
